Things To Note


How To Get XP


Xp can be gotten from wining battles


Beating an opponent in your rank or below: 1xp

Beating an opponent at least 2 ranks above you: 2xp

Beating an opponent before or at 5 turns: +1xp


Xp can also be bought from an admin at the rate of 5xp per ₦100(bonus may be given)


How To Get Crowns


Crowns are gotten by winning battles


Beating an opponent in your rank or a rank below: 30 crowns

Beating an opponent at least 2 ranks below yours: 25 crowns

Beating an opponent at least 2 ranks above you: 40 crowns

Beating an opponent before or at turn 5: +10 crowns


Note: You can run from a battle without losing crowns by using 2xp.

Crowns cannot be bought.


All Battle Types


1-on-1 Battle: This is a normal battle fought in the RPG group, where you gain normal xp and crowns. You do not die as a side effect of having your health reduced to zero.


2-on-2 Tag Team Battle: This is like a normal battle but fought with 2 players on a team instead of 1.


2-on-1 Handicap Battle: This is a variation of the 2-on-2 battle where 1 player faces 2 opponents.


Fusion Battle: This is a type of battle where 2 or more players fuse their health, cp and skills to face opponents who are also fused. In this battle, the fused skills of the opponents must be of the same skill type


Tournament Battle: This is a battle mode with different stages where players compete for a set prize.


Event Battle: This are rare battles created by the admins. Here, players team up to defeat a powerful foe. There are prizes if the battle is won, and repercussions if the battle is lost.


Beast Battle: This is a battle to gain a beast, crowns cannot be won from this battle, although they can be lost, more info in the Beasts section.


Death Battle: This is a battle that if lost, you need to pay a specific amount of xp or wait 3 days to be revived, dead players cannot compete in tournaments, events or any other battle. If this battle is won, the rewards are greater than a normal battle. A player can compete in 1 death battle every 2 weeks.


Training Battle: Similar to normal battle except there are no effects when lost or won.


Custom Battle: In this battle, the players set their own rules and play, xp and crowns may or may not be gained.


During Event & Beast Battles, you are allowed to use special gains called Senzu Beans & Energy capsules. During event battles you can use 2 of each while during beast battles you can only use 1 of each. To use these gains they must be bought before the battle. When you use these gains in the battle, you still play.


Senzu Bean: Gain 5 health. 1xp to buy.

Energy Capsule: Gain 5cp. 1xp to buy.


Character Card


A new character card can be bought for 60xp. This card must be from another race. You can switch between character cards to use during battles but cannot switch during a battle(unless you have a skill which can do that).


Other Notes


Modes can always be reactivated when deactivated, but you need to meet the requirements of using that mode first.

Mode activation and deactivation take a turn.

Health difference based moves cannot be used by clones.

Using a skill that makes opponent skip turns does not mean opponent cannot play in your turn.

Chakra fruit/Ki Boost/Magic Pill can only be used by a maximum of 2 clones at a time.
