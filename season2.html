Shonen Force: Season 2 - New Beginnings


Shonen Force is here with a brand new season poised to bring more action, events and tournaments.


Battle Pass(C) - An All New Currency


Introducing a new in-game currency to stand alongside the XP, this currency cannot be gotten by battling but can only be bought at the rate of ₦200 for 5C. This all new currency will give you access to buy some moves which would otherwise only be unlocked by achieving a certain rank, the ability to buy moves during tournament as well as senzu beans, the ability to have 1 beast battle a week for a specific amount & energy capsules during events and gives bonuses from winning matches.


A New Battle System


Introducing an all new battle system in Shonen Force. This new system encourages players to be more strategic in battle.


In the new system, all players are allowed to fight friendly battles with anyone up until they reach the third rank. After which, every battle they fight will be a special type of death battle - Ranked Battles.


In Ranked Battles, you are allowed to fight a maximum of 5 battles a day, although if you lose a battle, you are unable to fight any other battle for the rest of the day. You are allowed to fight one friendly battle every 3 days.
